How to Play

RPG Adventure Dungeon Crawler

Three floors. Five minutes. One shot at the leaderboard. XPL0R3R is a score-attack crawler with a persistent RPG layer — create a character, level up between runs, equip gear, and tackle harder dungeons as you grow. Surviving is just the entry fee. Every kill, every step, every second left on the clock feeds into your final score. Play clean, play fast, play aggressive.

The Run

• Descend 3 floors, retrieve the Chalice, and get out alive — you have 5 minutes

• Floors 1 & 2: find the Hole to go deeper. Floor 3: dominate the Dungeon Master — the Chalice appears on his death

• Finishing the run is the baseline. How you finish is what puts you on the leaderboard.

Controls

Keyboard

• [WASD / Arrows] — Move

• [1 / Z] — Swing flail — AOE hitting all 8 adjacent tiles at once

• [2 / X] — Health Potion  ·  [3 / C] — Armour Potion  ·  [4 / V] — Damage Potion

• [Esc] — Menu

Mobile

• D-pad to move — centre button swings the flail

• Potion buttons (Armour / Health / Damage) on the opposite side

• Swap sides in Settings → Controls → Left-Handed Layout

Combat

• The flail hits all 8 adjacent tiles — position yourself to catch multiple enemies in one swing

• Enemies retaliate instantly when struck — surrounded by three enemies means three hits back every swing

• Chain kills within 3 seconds to trigger a Combo — each kill in the chain earns bonus score

• Base flail damage: 18 + 3 per level. Damage Potions stack on top — use them before big fights

• Watch the Dungeon Master — he telegraphs heavy hits with a wind-up. Quick jabs come without warning.

Potions

• Health — +25 HP, can overheal. Floor drops only — not in chests. Save for emergencies; HP remaining at the end is worth score.

• Armour — +50 Armour (max 100). Each Armour point absorbs 2 damage — twice as efficient as raw HP. Prefer Armour over Health for staying power.

• Damage — permanent +10% damage per use, stacks every time, lasts the full run. The faster you kill, the better your combos and time bonus.

Enemies

• Slime — 20 HP · 3 DMG · +1 score  |  Skeleton — 40 HP · 6 DMG · +2 score  |  Ghost — 30 HP · 9 DMG · +3 score

• Stats shown are Floor 1 values — enemies get tougher each floor

• Clear a floor with no enemies left when you descend for a +25 bonus — worth hunting stragglers

• Dungeon Master — boss on Floor 3. High HP, high damage, two attack modes. Kill him to spawn the Chalice.

Keys & Chests

• Each floor has one Key — pick it up to unlock the Chest

• Chests offer a choice of two — pick the one that fits your run:

— Armour Potion: +50 Armour (absorbs 100 damage total)

— Damage Potion: +10% permanent damage — pick this if you're chasing combos

• Cracked walls can be broken with the flail — some conceal a hidden chamber with guaranteed loot inside (more common on deeper floors)

• Lucky Charm ★ — a rare golden star found in the dungeon or hidden chambers; the next flail hit instantly kills any non-boss enemy

Terrain

• Water — impassable. Rerouting costs steps — every detour hurts your Efficiency Bonus.

• Lava — first step deals 90% of your current HP. A second step kills you. Never cross twice.

• Moss — harmless. Walk freely.

Scoring

• Kills: Slime +1 · Skeleton +2 · Ghost +3 — plus combo bonus for chained kills within 3s

• Floor descent: +50 per floor reached  |  Floor Clear: +25 for descending with zero enemies alive

• Chalice pickup: +250

• Time Bonus: +1 per second remaining — move with purpose

• Efficiency Bonus: full +120 for ≤200 steps, then drops 0.2 per step — straight lines beat backtracking

• HP Bonus: +0.5 per HP remaining — finishing healthy matters

• Level Bonus: triangular scaling per level-up — higher levels are worth progressively more

• Score cap: 999. A perfect run requires killing everything, levelling high, finishing fast, and arriving with full HP. Good luck.

Levelling

• EXP is separate from score — killing enemies fills your EXP bar

• Each level grants +3 flail damage and 5% damage reduction (capped at 50% at Level 11)

• Higher levels mean faster kills, less damage taken, and a bigger Level Bonus on your final score — killing everything pays off
